Issuance & Redemption of UPI Vouchers

Step 1: Business

Business manager uses Cotodel’s Platform to select relevant category spends, enter voucher amount and define time validity to issue UPI Vouchers powered by e-RUPI to each driver partner

Step 2: Driver Partner

All UPI Vouchers issued to Driver's number get aggreagted in e-RUPI section of their UPI Apps.

Step 3: Driver Partner

Driver scans Merchant's existing QR Code to make payment using e-RUPI Voucher on their existing UPI Apps.

Step 4: Driver Partner

Basis Merchant eligibility, relevant UPI e-RUPI Voucher pops-up automatically for payment at time of transaction.

e-RUPI is a digital payment solution launched by the Government of India in August 2021 to facilitate cashless and contactless transactions for a locked purpose. Developed by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), e-RUPI is a QR code or SMS string-based electronic voucher that is sent directly to the beneficiary’s mobile phone. It is a prepaid, purpose-specific voucher that can only be used for designated services such as fuel, meal, or travel, ensuring that funds are utilized exactly as intended. The system does not require the beneficiary to have a bank account, digital payment app, or internet access, making it especially useful for those with limited access to financial infrastructure. The vouchers are linked to the recipient’s identity and mobile number, ensuring a secure and leak-proof transfer of funds.
