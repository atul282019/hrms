About Us

Who we are

Cotodel is committed to redefining how your organisation approaches payments, compliances and employee-related tasks. No matter the size, we want to help you leverage the power of AI to reduce your administrative burdens and empower your employees with better incentives and technologies!

What we do

Gone are the days when your Finance and HR functions had to be burdened with managing multiple systems for payments, subscription management and compliances. Cotodel is here to change this for you - empowering you with the one-stop your office needs!

We are the first to offer UPI-enabled flexi-benefits with prepaid vouchers that can be redeemed using your UPI applications. No complications of maintaining paper or digital bills, or online filings. Your employees choose what they want as part of the bouquet of benefits you offer, and we help implement it seamlessly. Get ahead of the market with e-RUPI Vouchers for your all your office and employee related needs.
