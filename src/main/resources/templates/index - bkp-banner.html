About Us

Who we are

Cotodel is committed to delivering an operating system that your office really needs. No matter what is the size of your organisation, we want employees to have a HR Tech system that they actually use!

What we do

Gone are the days when HR systems were just meant to check your salary slips or mark your daily attendance! Cotodel is here to change this for you - the supreme operating system for your office!

Employees want an OS that solves all of their needs in a seamless and simple manner but the current systems do not solve for this. From automated onboarding to a payroll management system that caters to all compliances, we also let companies issues prepaid E-RUPI vouchers that are people and person specific.

Remember those expenses that you have still not filed because of the cumbersome process? Our OCR verified expenses and UPI-enabled benefits solve for that as well!
