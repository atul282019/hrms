Fuel Cards vs. UPI Digital Vouchers

UPI Digital Vouchers empowering Fleet Expense Management

Paras Gupta & Vidit Sidana

CotoPay May 15, 2025 4 min read

Managing fuel expenses is a core operational challenge for fleet managers. While fuel cards have been around for a long time, they come with several limitations. UPI Digital Vouchers are a new-age payment method which can act as an alternative mode of payment for fleets which are operating their fuel spends either via cash or fuel cards currently.

Read on for a comparison between Fuel cards and UPI Digital Vouchers and why UPI Digital Vouchers are emerging as the smarter choice for Fleet owners!

Availability : Go Where Your Fleet Goes

Fuel cards are limited to select partner fuel stations, creating inefficiencies when drivers deviate from routes or enter remote areas. A driver running low on fuel may need to detour significantly just to find an accepted station. In contrast, UPI Digital Vouchers offer universal acceptance at any UPI-enabled petrol pump â€” now covering most fuel stations across India. This enables faster refueling, eliminates detours, and enhances fleet productivity and route efficiency.

Additionally, fuel cards often rely on POS machines that may be out of service, not recharged, or lack network connectivity â€” especially common in peripheral areas or highways where commercial vehicles typically operate. This leads to transaction failures and delays. UPI Digital Vouchers, however, leverage mobile-based UPI apps that work seamlessly even in low-connectivity zones, ensuring smooth, real-time transactions and uninterrupted fleet operations.


Hidden Fees: : Say Goodbye to Unnecessary Costs

Fuel cards often come with joining fees, renewal charges, POS terminal fees, and sometimes even per-transaction fees. Over time, these add up and eat into your operating budget. In contrast, UPI Digital Vouchers come with zero hidden charges. What you see is what you pay.

Fuel cards are limited to fuel stations, offering minimal control over how and where funds are used. This level of granularity ensures tight control, reduces misuse, and enhances transparency.

Moreover, UPI Digital Vouchers are not restricted to fuel expenses alone. Fleet managers can configure vouchers to be used across other essential categories like toll payments, vehicle maintenance, food, and lodging â€” depending on the operational needs of the fleet. This makes UPI Digital Vouchers a highly versatile and scalable solution.

Seamless Onboarding: : Save Time, Go Paperless

Fuel card issuance typically requires paperwork, KYC verification, and waiting periods, slowing down operations, especially for businesses with large or mobile fleets. UPI Digital Vouchers simplify this process with digital onboarding. Fleet managers can instantly issue vouchers to drivers via mobile numbers, eliminating the need for physical cards or complex documentation. This is ideal for gig economy models and logistics startups that need to scale their fleets quickly and efficiently.
